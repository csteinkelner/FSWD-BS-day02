Characters

Mei Kusakabe

Mei Kusakabe, sister of Satsuki Kusakabe and daughter of Tatsuo Kusakabe and Yasuko Kusakabe was born in Tokyo in May 1953.
She is incredibly lively and active as well as extremely curious.

She is the first to meet Totoro and does so by falling down a hole in a tree root after following smaller totoros excitedly. Mei isn't the least bit scared of the new creature she has found even when it opens its huge mouth to yawn. Instead she just opens her mouth back. Mei does have a caring side and decides to go to the hospital with the sweet corn she picked from Granny's garden. However she ends up getting lost on the way and gets very upset

Satsuki Kusakabe

Satsuki Kusakabe (Kusakabe Satsuki) also known Satsuko is one of the protagonists of My Neighbor Totoro. She is the sister of Mei in My Neighbor Totoro. Satsuki can be irritable towards her sister Mei, for example at the end of the film when they find out that their mother won't be coming home from the hospital for the weekend, she scolded her sister for getting upset. Satsuki likes to think of herself as grown-up and caring for her sister. She is very lively, although not quite as lively as Mei. Satsuki is a good sister on the whole and looks after Mei to the best of her ability despite being upset about their ill mother. She is also very kind and responsible overall.

Tatsuo Kusakabe

Tatsuo Kusakabe is the father or Satsuki and Mei Kusakabe, and the husband of Yasuko Kusakabe (in hospital).

Tatsuo Kusakabe is personable and works in a college as a professor of Archaeology. He is a very good father, and does his best to keep Mei and Satsuki happy when their mother is in the hospital. He believes in the imagination of his daughters, and agrees with their idea of Totoro.

Totoro

Totoro is a forest spirit and protects the forest and has powers over the wind and plants.
He helps Mei and Satsuki.
